Current Calibration Algorithm

Summer Internship: Developed a current calibration algorithm for the AS7352 chip, a 14-channel multispectral sensor. The algorithm dynamically adjusts the current supplied to 10 individual LEDs to maximize output across the photodiode channels. This has enabled the smart ring to adapt to a wide range of skin tones effectively. The algorithm is highly efficient, completing the LED current optimization process in under 10 seconds.

Formula Student Accumulator Controller Module (ACM)

Designed a 6-layer battery management ECU with 600 V isolation, HV sensing, and isoSPI links; optimized signal integrity through controlled impedance routing and isolation practices to ensure robust HV monitoring and control. Manufactured spare ACM PCB responsible for managing the high-voltage system for a FSAE car. This involved hand soldering over 400+ SMD components. Further, Testing was done with custom C++ embedded firmware.

Formula Student STM32 based Front Controller R&D

Conducted R&D to choose a STM32 based MCU for the 2026 season. We decided to use the STM32G47 series. This dev board includes USB programming, USB->UART bridges (USB Micro and USB C), CAN controllers, LED drivers, STM32 based internal buffer experimentation and 5V tolerant pins to prevent the use of level shifters. Overall we saw a 30% reduction in BOM cost.
